Custard Recepie:

origin: ancient-rome, France, england

taste: creamy, sweet, aromatic, silky texture

ocassion: special feasts, festivals, family-gatherings

served with: warm-baked pies,fresh fruits, classic puddings, after lunch

Ingredients:

  • Whole Milk: 2 to 2 ½ cups (approx. 500–600 ml)
  • Large Egg Yolks: 3 to 4 yolks (for a richer texture)
  • Granulated Sugar: ½ cup (approx. 100g), or adjust to taste
  • Vanilla Extract: 1 to 2 teaspoons (or 1 whole vanilla bean)
  • Cornstarch (Thickener): 3 tablespoons (essential for stovetop thickening; optional for baked versions)
  • Salt: A pinch (roughly ⅛ to ¼ teaspoon)
  • Unsalted Butter: 1 to 2 tablespoons (optional, for extra shine and richness)
