Ingredients:

  • Whole chicken
  • Unsalted butter
  • Kosher salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lemon
  • Garlic
  • Fresh rosemary
  • Fresh thyme
  • Fresh sage
  • Yellow onion
  • Olive oil
  • Chicken stock

Method:

To prepare a classic herb-butter roast chicken, start by preheating your oven to 425째F (220째C) and ensuring the bird is at room temperature. The most critical step for achieving crispy skin is to pat the chicken completely dry with paper towels inside and out. Once dried, generously season the internal cavity with kosher salt and pepper, then stuff it with a halved lemon, a head of garlic cut crosswise, and a bundle of fresh rosemary, thyme, and sage. To ensure succulent meat, rub a generous amount of softened unsalted butter both over the skin and underneath the skin of the breast, followed by a final sprinkle of salt and pepper on the exterior. Place the chicken on a bed of sliced yellow onions and olive oil in a roasting pan, or on a roasting rack, and cook for approximately 50 to 70 minutes. Use a meat thermometer to ensure the thickest part of the thigh reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C). Finally, remove the chicken from the oven and let it rest for at least 15 to 20 minutes before carving; this allows the juices to redistribute, ensuring every slice is moist and flavorful. For an extra touch of flavor, you can deglaze the roasting pan with chicken stock to create a simple, savory jus to serve alongside the meat.
